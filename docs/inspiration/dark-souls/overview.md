# Dark Souls Inspiration

> "next is dark souls. this is incredibly precise worldbuilding and level design, difficult but simple combat (just spacing, timing, and dodging) really interesting enemies and environment, great replayability. just the feeling the vibe is truly unparallelled"

## Core Highlights
*   **Interconnected Level Design:** Meticulously crafted worlds where shortcuts and verticality create a sense of place and discovery.
*   **Precision Combat:** A high-stakes system focused on "Spacing, Timing, and Dodging" rather than complex button combos.
*   **The "Vibe" (Atmosphere):** A melancholic, mysterious, and oppressive world that tells its story through environment rather than cutscenes.
*   **High-Stakes Progression:** Risk-reward mechanics where death has consequences, but mastery feels earned.
*   **Enemy Variety & Design:** Unique, grotesque, and challenging enemies that require learning patterns to defeat.
*   **Replayability:** Deep build variety and secret paths that encourage multiple playthroughs.

## Research Findings
*   **Diegetic Storytelling:** Lore is hidden in item descriptions and environmental details, rewarding player curiosity.
*   **I-Frame Dodge:** The core defensive mechanic relies on "Invincibility Frames" during a roll, making timing the primary skill.
*   **Metroidvania Layout:** Levels often loop back on themselves, using locked doors or elevators to connect distant areas once progress is made.
