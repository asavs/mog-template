## Core Highlights
*   **Total Destructibility:** The "destroy any block" philosophy provides ultimate player agency and makes the world feel truly interactive.
*   **The "Mines" Satisfaction:** The tactile and auditory feedback (SFX) of mining ores is a core hook.
*   **Procedural Generation:** An infinite, procedurally generated open world that encourages exploration and discovery.
*   **Creative Construction:** Building houses and structures as a form of self-expression and progression.
*   **Projectile Physics:** The "feel" of the bow—tension, arc, and impact—is highly satisfying.
*   **Identity & Customization:** Custom skins allow for high player expression and social identity.

## Research Findings
*   **Voxel Tech:** Minecraft's world is built on a voxel grid, allowing for efficient storage and manipulation of massive, destructible terrains.
*   **Sound Design:** The specific "clink" and "pop" sounds of mining are iconic and scientifically satisfying (ASMR-adjacent).
*   **Progression Loop:** The "Mine -> Craft -> Upgrade" loop provides a clear, repeating sense of achievement.
