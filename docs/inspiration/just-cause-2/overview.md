# Just Cause 2 Inspiration

> "if we go in order of my childhood next would be just cause 2. this game was awesome because you could always cause chaos there were very few npcs who were protected, so there was a sense of morals at all times. the other great thiunga bout it was the movement, particularly the grappling hook. you could use the grappling hook plus the parachute to fly around. incredibly satisfying"

## Core Highlights
*   **The Grapple-Parachute Combo:** A high-speed traversal system using a grappling hook to slingshot the player while parachuting.
*   **Systemic Chaos:** A world where almost everything is interactable or destructible, creating a playground for emergent gameplay.
*   **Moral Freedom:** Lack of "protected" NPCs gives players full agency (and responsibility) over their actions in the world.

## Research Findings
*   **Traversal Physics:** The "slingshot" effect relies on maintaining momentum between the grapple pull and the parachute lift.
*   **Chaos Points:** JC2 uses a "Chaos" metric to track progression, rewarding the player for destroying infrastructure and completing objectives.
*   **Emergent Interaction:** Tethering objects/NPCs to each other or to vehicles creates endless creative possibilities for combat and movement.
